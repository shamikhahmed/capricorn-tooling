# Capricorn Apple-Grade Voice Guide

## Principles
- **Lead with outcome**, not features. "Know your net worth" beats "Portfolio analytics module."
- **Short lines.** Headlines ≤6 words. Subheads one sentence. Body ≤2 sentences per block.
- **Active voice.** "Your data stays on your device" not "Data is stored locally."
- **No hype words** without proof: revolutionary, AI-powered (unless LLM), seamless, cutting-edge.
- **Smart Assistant** for rules-based logic. Never claim LLM unless integrated.
- **Distinct per app** — VaultCap is Swiss precision; PulseCap is kinetic; ScentCap is warm editorial.

## Headline patterns
- `[Product].` + `[Verb] [object].` — "VaultCap. Own your ledger."
- Problem slide: `[Category] is [broken adjective].` — "Finance is fragmented."
- Promise: One sentence starting with "You" or imperative.
